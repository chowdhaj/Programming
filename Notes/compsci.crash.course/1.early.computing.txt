Notes
-----

- Computers are the building blocks of today's world; they control everything
- Earliest computing device was the abacus. Its a calculator that can add, subtract, and store numbers
- Step Reckoner was the first device to do all four arithmetic operations (Add, Subtract, Multiply, & Divide)
- Charles Babbage pioneered the Difference Engine, a computer to calculate polynomials
- Babbage is considered the father of computing
- Herman Hollerith founded the Tabulating Machine Company which later merged with other machine makers in 1924 to become IBM Co.
